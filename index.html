Keanu Reeves is a Canadian actor, director, producer, and musician. He is most known for his roles in such films as the Matrix Triliogy, Point Break, Speed, and the John Wick franchise. Not only is Keanu one of Hollywoods biggest and well known action stars, he is also known for his philanthropy and the kindness he shares with co-workers and fans.
